About Corellian

Our Story

At Corellian, we understand the critical importance of seamless and efficient rigging and construction operations for the successful completion of major projects. Established in 2009 by industry veterans Anthony Micallef and Gary Jefferson, our company was founded on the unwavering mission to provide the highest standards of rigging services.

Our team of highly skilled professionals boasts a comprehensive range of expertise, including metal fabrication, plastering, carpentry, plumbing, and bricklaying. This diverse skillset enables us to tackle any challenge on any construction site with confidence and precision.

Corellian takes pride in our ability to safely and efficiently relocate loads up to 50 tonnes in weight, as well as delicate and sensitive equipment such as data servers. Our whole-package approach to rigging has earned us a reputation as an industry leader.

Our Services Overview

Expert Rigging Services

Corellian provides comprehensive rigging solutions combining professional expertise in rigging, dogging, craneage and traffic control. We specialize in the safe installation and relocation of heavy machinery and delicate equipment.

  • HVAC equipment installation and relocation
  • Generators, chillers, boilers, and mechanical plant
  • Structural steel erection and metalwork
  • Wind turbine assembly and installation
  • Materials handling solutions

Our experienced team handles everything from delicate switchboards to massive industrial equipment, ensuring safe transport through any space to any location.

Comprehensive Demolition

Our demolition services cover everything from base-building to detailed services removal. We specialize in the careful demolition and removal of mechanical systems and building components.

  • Chiller and boiler removal with gas decontamination
  • HVAC systems and ductwork dismantling
  • Hydraulic and refrigerant pipework removal
  • Concrete plinths and structural elements
  • Asbestos decontamination and certification

We provide complete penetration services and can demolish masonry, partition walls, suspended ceilings, and all types of building materials with precision and safety.

Precision Building Works

From complete plant room retrofits to concrete work and temporary structures, our building services cover all architectural trade requirements. We provide both base-building and fit-out solutions.

  • Concrete plinths and structural foundations
  • Roof installations and penetrations
  • Partition walls and ceiling systems
  • Metalwork fabrication and installation
  • Security systems and access solutions

Our comprehensive approach includes design and construct services, ensuring all architectural obligations are met for HVAC and electrical services contracts.

Our Directors

Meet the experienced leaders who founded and continue to drive Corellian's success.

Anthony Micallef

General Manager & Co-Founder

Industry veteran with over 30 years of experience in rigging and construction operations.

Anthony co-founded Corellian in 2009 with a vision to revolutionize the rigging industry through comprehensive, whole-package solutions. With over three decades of hands-on experience, he has personally overseen some of Australia's most challenging rigging projects.

His expertise spans across all aspects of heavy lifting, from delicate medical equipment installations to massive industrial machinery relocations. Anthony's commitment to safety and innovation has established Corellian as a trusted name in the industry.

Under his leadership, Corellian has grown from a small specialized team to Australia's premier rigging service provider, maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety across all operations.

Gary Jefferson

Operations Director & Co-Founder

Expert in operational excellence and strategic project management with 25+ years experience.

Gary Jefferson brings a unique combination of technical expertise and strategic thinking to Corellian's operations. As co-founder, he has been instrumental in developing the company's systematic approach to complex rigging challenges.

His background in engineering and project management ensures that every Corellian project is executed with precision, on time, and within budget. Gary's focus on continuous improvement has led to numerous innovations in rigging techniques and safety protocols.

He oversees all major project operations and maintains Corellian's reputation for delivering impossible-seeming projects with reliability and professionalism that clients have come to expect.
