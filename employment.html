General Labourer / Rigger

Currently OPEN for applications from June 2025

Are you looking for a challenging and rewarding career in the construction industry? Have a look at our General Labourer / Rigger position! As a General Labourer, you'll have the opportunity to work on a wide range of projects, including demolition and rigging assistance work, associated building works, and site maintenance. You'll also have the chance to operate a variety of construction equipment as you gather training and licences, such as forklifts, HIABs, EWPs, and telehandlers. We have a fleet of utes and a handful of trucks, so alongside loading and unloading, yard and workshop duties, there are opportunities for MR and HR licence holders to spend some time driving trucks to site.

But that's not all – as you gain experience and prove yourself on the job, you'll have the opportunity to "learn the ropes" and become a Corellian specialist Rigger. As a specialist Rigger, you'll take on additional responsibilities to apply our unique industry-leading rigging techniques and have the chance to advance your career even further. And if you're ready to take on a leadership role, you may even have the rewarding opportunity to become a Team Leader, where you'll manage and coordinate your team's work, ensure compliance with safety procedures, and assist with project and contract management.

At our company, we value hard work, dedication, and a commitment to safety. That's why we offer competitive pay, benefits, and opportunities for growth and advancement. If you're ready to take the next step in your career and join a dynamic and supportive team, apply now for our General Labourer / Rigger position. We can't wait to see what you'll achieve with us!

Complete and download the application form here and send it with your resume to our HR@Corellian.com.au email address or upload it with the contact form below.
